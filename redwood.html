Welcome to Redwood High School, Larkspur

About Us

Redwood High School, located in Larkspur, California, is a comprehensive high school known for its strong academic programs, exceptional extracurricular activities, and supportive community.

Our mission is to provide a challenging and engaging educational experience that empowers students to become lifelong learners and responsible global citizens.

Academics

At Redwood, we offer a wide range of rigorous academic courses to prepare students for success in college and beyond. Our dedicated faculty are committed to providing personalized support and fostering a love of learning in every student.

Extracurriculars

Our school offers a variety of extracurricular activities, including sports teams, clubs, and performing arts groups. Whether you're interested in athletics, community service, or creative pursuits, there's something for everyone at Redwood.
