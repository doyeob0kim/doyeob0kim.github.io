Welcome to Redwood High School

Located in the beautiful city of Larkspur, California, Redwood High School is dedicated to fostering an environment of academic excellence, community engagement, and personal growth.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire, educate, and empower our students to become critical thinkers and compassionate leaders who will make a positive impact in the world.

History

Established in 1958, Redwood High School has a proud tradition of excellence in academics, athletics, and the arts. We take pride in our vibrant community and diverse student body.
